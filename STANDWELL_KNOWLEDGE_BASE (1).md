## What Standwell Is

Standwell is a brand presentation company. We help businesses show up well in person — at events, conferences, retail activations, offices, and anywhere a brand needs to make an impression. We do this through portable displays, custom backdrops, and branded environments that are well-made, delivered on time, and simple to order.

We work directly with marketing teams, brand managers, and business owners who need professional results without agency overhead or complexity.

## What We Offer

Standwell offers three core product categories:

**1. Displays & Banner Stands**
Portable, professional signage for events, conferences, lobbies, and retail. Retractable banner stands, tabletop displays, and freestanding panels.

**2. Backdrops & Fabric Displays**
Step-and-repeat backdrops, tension fabric pop-up displays, and printed booth walls. Ideal for event presence, photo moments, and branded environments.

**3. Branded Event Kits**
Complete, coordinated setups for events and activations — displays, table covers, banner stands, and accessories bundled for a cohesive brand presence.

## How It Works

1. **Get a quote** — Submit a request at standwell.com or email hello@standwell.com with your event date, size, and what you're looking for.
2. **We recommend the right product** — We match you to the right format, size, and finish for your use case and budget.
3. **Approve your design** — We collect your brand assets, build a digital proof, and get your sign-off before anything goes to production.
4. **We produce and ship** — Orders ship in 5–7 business days standard. Rush options available.
5. **You show up ready** — Products ship directly to you or your venue. We follow up after to make sure everything landed right.

**Payment:** 100% upfront before production begins — standard for all custom printed work.
